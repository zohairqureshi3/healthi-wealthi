What is HealthiWealthi™?

If I would ask, "What's really important to you?" health and wealth are very likely on the very top. Health is important because without health we have nothing. Wealth (or financial security, a stable income, feeling prosperous, or whatever term you use) is equally vital for fulfilling your life's dreams and passions.

At HealthiWealthi™ we believe that you should be "paid and rewarded for living healthy".

"You never change things by fighting the existing reality. To change something, build a new model that makes the existing model obsolete." Buckminster Fuller.

The Chronic Disease Pandemic

1+ billion people worldwide suffer from chronic diseases like heart disease, obesity, diabetes, pain, depression, anxiety caused by unhealthy lifestyle habits. Because healthcare is mainly focused on managing disease, we firmly believe a new innovative, scalable healthcare 3.0 platform needs to be built that provides easy and affordable access to Lifestyle Medicine and Health Coaching. Lifestyle is medicine with extensive research showing that the majority of chronic health issues can be stopped or reversed through comprehensive lifestyle improvements.

Our Vision

Everyone has affordable access to Lifestyle Medicine and Health Coach practitioners.

Our Mission

Unlock human potential - inspire millions to live healthier, happier, longer aligned with their life's purpose guided by certified health coaches living their own dream.

Think "Spotify®" For Healthy Living

Health Coaches & Practitioners

Health providers are the main contributors & proactive drivers of the platform. They are paid for services provided to members (clients) and earn RXHEAL tokens and rewards.

Personal & Family Memberships

Individuals and families will receive RXHEAL rewards for group and personal consultations, engaging with their personal coach, and improving their lifestyle habits.

Teams

HealthiWealthi™ provides doctors, clinics, hospitals, health centers, & corporations an easy-to-integrate way to add preventive & health coaching services without the need to hire expensive staff or change their workflow.

School Teachers

School teachers join our platform to teach kids and youth how to live healthy and wealthy by practicing conscious, empowering lifestyle habits.
